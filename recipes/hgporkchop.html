Honey Garlic Pork Chops

These are some of the best pork chops you will ever grill and eat. Just listen to what a few reviews left by families and friends who have tried our Honey Garlic Pork Chops.

"I don't normally go ga-ga over a recipe, even if I like it. I have to say, however, that these were the best pork chops I have ever eaten. I chose to bake the chops due to weather. My wife even told me to go online and give it five stars. No argument from me!!. -Bobby Hill

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Ketchup
  • 2-2/3 tablespoons Honey
  • 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 Cloves Crushed Garlic
  • Salt & Pepper to Taste
  • 6 4ounce Pork Chops (1in. Thick)

Directions

  • Step 1: Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil grate
  • Step 2: Whisk honey, soy sauce, and garlic on a bowl to make a glaze.
  • Step 3: Sear the pork chops on both sides on the preheated grill. Lightly brush glaze onto each side of the chops as they cook; grill until no longer pink in the center, about 7 to 9 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
  • Step 4: Enjoy! Thats how easy it is.
