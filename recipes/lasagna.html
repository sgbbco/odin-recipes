Grandmas famous 20 Cheese Lasagna. She started to call it 20 cheese Lasagna when everyone wanted to pick which cheese to go in between each layer. In reality it likely would have been gross had she put 20 different cheeses inside. However,she always told us that each persons recommended cheese was inside.

She was great like that and always worked to keep our smiles full as our bellies.

Ingredients

  • 16 Ounce Lasagna Noodles
  • 4 Cups Ricotta Cheese
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/4 Cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper to Taste
  • 1 teaspoon Olive Oil
  • 3 Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 32 Ounces Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • 2 Cups Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  • Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 Degrees F (175 C)
  • Step 2: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and lay noodles flat on foil to cool.
  • Step 3: Mix ricotta, eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; mix well
  • Step 4: Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat; add garlic and saute 2 minutes. Stir in spaghetti sauce and italian seasoning until warmed through, stirring occasionally, 2 to 5 minutes.
  • Step 5: Spread 1/2 cup of sauce on the bottom of a 9 x 13-inch baking dish. Cover with a layer of noodles. Spread 1/2 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles; top with another layer of noodles. Pour 1 1/2 cups of sauce of noodles and spread the remaining ricotta over the sauce. Top with remaining noodles and sauce; sprinkle with remaining mozzarella. Cover with a greased sheet of foil.
  • Step 6: Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, about 45 minutes.
